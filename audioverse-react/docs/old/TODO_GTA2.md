# TODO: GTA 2 — Top-Down Open World Action RPG

## Concept
A top-down open-world action game inspired by GTA 2. Very deep and expansive: procedurally generated cities with RPG progression, faction systems, property ownership, business building, and territory control. Multiple world settings (Medieval, Sci-Fi, Post-Apocalypse, Modern) with dramatically different vehicles, weapons, and gameplay flavor. Real-time action, exploration, and emergent gameplay.

## Game Modes

### Single Player
- **Main Story**: Multi-act narrative per world setting (30+ hours each)
- **Sandbox**: Open world to explore, no story pressure, pure emergent gameplay
- **Career Mode**: Rise from nobody to crime lord — reputation-driven progression
- **Challenge Missions**: Specific vehicle/combat/heist challenges with star ratings
- **Business Tycoon**: Focus on property acquisition and business management

### Couch Co-op / Split Screen (up to 4 players)
- **Co-op Story**: Play the main story together, shared wanted level
- **VS Deathmatch**: Arena maps, weapons scattered, last person standing
- **Race Mode**: Vehicle races through the city streets
- **Territory War**: Each player leads a faction, compete for blocks
- **Heist Co-op**: Plan and execute multi-stage heists together

### Online Multiplayer
- **Persistent World**: MMO-lite open world with other players (up to 64)
- **Faction Wars**: Guilds/gangs competing for territory control
- **Co-op Heists**: Matchmaking for multi-player robbery missions
- **Competitive**: Deathmatch, races, capture the flag
- **Trading**: Player-to-player property and item trading
- **Seasonal Events**: Time-limited game modes and world events

## Gameplay Mechanics

### Movement & Combat
- **Top-down view** with smooth 360° rotation
- **On foot**: Run, sprint, roll/dodge, stealth crouch
- **Vehicles**: Cars, trucks, motorcycles, boats, helicopters (per-world variants)
- **Weapons**: Melee (bat, sword, wrench), pistol, shotgun, SMG, rifle, explosives, special
- **Combat**: Auto-aim assist + manual fine aim, cover system behind objects
- **Wanted Level**: 1-5 stars, escalating police/guard response

### Open World Systems
- **Procedural Cities**: Algorithm-generated districts with hand-crafted POIs
- **Traffic & Pedestrians**: Dynamic NPC life, vehicles obey traffic (mostly)
- **Day/Night Cycle**: Affects NPC behavior, shop hours, crime rates
- **Weather**: Rain, snow, storms — affect driving and visibility
- **Interiors**: Enter shops, buildings, safehouses, businesses

### RPG Progression
- **Character Stats**: Driving, shooting, melee, stealth, charisma, endurance
- **Skills**: Lockpicking, hacking, persuasion, repair, cooking, first aid
- **Perks**: Every 5 levels, game-changing abilities
- **Reputation**: Per-faction standing — allies give quests, enemies attack on sight
- **Wanted Heat**: Persistent memory of crimes per district

### Property & Business
- **Property Types**: Safehouses, garages, shops, warehouses, estates, penthouses
- **Businesses**: Restaurants, car shops, weapon stores, nightclubs — generate passive income
- **Upgrades**: Security, decorations, capacity, staff quality
- **Territory**: Control city blocks — earn protection money, defend from rivals
- **Investment**: Buy/sell properties, stock-like market for business shares

### Vehicles
- **Acquisition**: Steal from streets, buy from dealers, earn from missions, find rare spawns
- **Customization**: Paint, engine, armor, weapons (mounted guns, spikes, nitro)
- **Garage**: Store and maintain collection
- **Classes**: Compact, sedan, sports, muscle, truck, motorcycle, armored, special

### Faction System
- Multiple competing factions per world
- Do jobs for factions → gain standing → unlock exclusive missions and perks
- Helping one faction may anger another
- Faction story arcs with unique missions and rewards
- Player can create their own faction (online) or remain independent

## Worlds & Settings

### 🏙️ Modern City
- Contemporary setting, realistic vehicles and weapons
- Factions: Mafia, Street Gang, Corporation, Vigilantes, Police (corrupt)
- Vehicles: Cars, bikes, boats, helicopters
- Special: Stock market, nightlife, street racing scene

### ⚔️ Medieval Kingdom
- Fantasy medieval town with horses, carts, castles
- Factions: Crown, Thieves Guild, Rebel Alliance, Merchant League, Dark Cult
- Weapons: Swords, bows, crossbows, magic staves, siege equipment
- Vehicles: Horses, carts, boats, war elephants
- Special: Castle sieges, jousting tournaments, dragon encounters

### 🚀 Sci-Fi Megacity
- Cyberpunk metropolis with hover vehicles and energy weapons
- Factions: Corporation, Hackers, Resistance, Android Rights, Security Force
- Weapons: Plasma guns, laser rifles, EMP grenades, hacking tools
- Vehicles: Hover cars, speeders, drones, mech suits
- Special: Hacking minigames, cybernetic upgrades, virtual reality zones

### 🏚️ Post-Apocalypse
- Wasteland settlements connected by dangerous highways
- Factions: Settlers, Raiders, Cult, Nomad Caravans, Tech Brotherhood
- Weapons: Improvised melee, scrap guns, crossbows, flamethrowers
- Vehicles: Armored trucks, dune buggies, motorcycles, muscle cars with spikes
- Special: Scavenging, radiation zones, water as currency, fortification building

## Monetization

### Currency 1: Cash (💵)
- Primary earned from missions, businesses, crimes, selling loot
- Used for: weapons, vehicles, property purchases, staff salaries, vehicle modifications

### Currency 2: Influence (🌟)
- Earned from faction missions, territory control, competitive wins, achievements
- Used for: premium vehicle blueprints, exclusive property cosmetics, character outfits, weapon skins, faction-specific perks

### Currency 3: Notoriety (💀)
- Earned from high-profile heists, PvP kills, wanted level survival streaks, world events, community notoriety rankings
- Used for: legendary vehicles, exclusive weapon mods, custom safehouse themes, dark market access, criminal empire features

### Monetizable Content
- Vehicle skin collections per world setting
- Character outfit packs (per world themed)
- Property interior decoration themes
- Custom weapon skins and effects
- Radio station music DLC (per world setting)
- Additional world setting DLC
- Custom horn sounds and vehicle effects

## Technical Considerations
- Procedural city generation with hand-placed landmarks
- Vehicle physics: drifting, collisions, damage model, top-down perspective challenges
- NPC AI: traffic, pedestrians, police response, faction behavior
- Persistence: property ownership, faction state, character progress across sessions
- Network: 64 player world hosting with area-of-interest culling
- Save system: extensive — character, properties, vehicles, faction state, inventory
- Rendering: efficient tile-based map with dynamic lighting
- Audio: per-world radio stations with song variety
- Mod support: custom missions, vehicles, characters, world districts
